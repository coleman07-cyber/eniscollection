OUR STORY

Enis Collection started in 2022 in Cape Coast with a simple goal. Help women feel confident and comfortable in what they wear. The brand began on a small scale with carefully selected thrift pieces and handmade outfits. Each piece was chosen with attention to style and quality. As more women discovered the brand, customers shared their experience with others and the community around Enis Collection grew. Today, Enis Collection continues to offer stylish thrift pieces and handmade outfits for the modern woman, with a focus on comfort, style, and confidence..
