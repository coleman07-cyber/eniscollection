Our Story

Enis Collection started in 2022 in Cape Coast with a simple goal — help women feel confident and comfortable in what they wear. The brand began with carefully selected thrift pieces and handmade outfits, each chosen with attention to style and quality. As more women discovered the brand, the community grew. Today, Enis Collection continues to offer stylish pieces for the modern woman, with a focus on comfort, style, and confidence.
